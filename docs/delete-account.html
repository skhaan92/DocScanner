Delete Account & Data

Last updated: June 2026

What data is deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your Google account sign-in association
  • Your name, email address, and profile photo stored in the app
  • Your subscription and login status
  • Any app preferences and settings tied to your account

Data that is not deleted (stored locally on your device only):

  • Scanned documents and PDFs saved on your device — you can delete these manually from within the app or your file manager
Option 1 — Recommended

Delete account from within the app

You can sign out and remove your account data directly from the app:

  1. Open Scanner - Scan PDF & Docs on your device
  2. Tap the menu icon (☰) in the top-left corner to open the drawer
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap "Logout"
  4. Your account data (name, email, photo, login status) is immediately cleared from the app
⚠️ Signing out removes your account association from this app. To fully revoke Google Sign-In access, also remove the app from your Google Account permissions (see Option 2).
Option 2 — Full Revocation

Revoke Google account access

To fully remove Scanner's access to your Google account:

  1. Go to myaccount.google.com/permissions
  2. Find Scanner - Scan PDF & Docs in the list
  3. Click on it and select "Remove Access"

This revokes all OAuth permissions granted to the app from Google's side.
